Frequently Asked Questions

How are the sessions conducted?
All sessions are conducted online via our secure platform with video, audio, and interactive whiteboard. No special software needed - just a computer with internet access.
What if my child doesn't like their tutor?
We'll match you with a different tutor at no extra cost. Finding the right personality match is important to us.
Can I change or cancel my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with no penalties. We're confident you'll love our service, but there's no obligation.
How do you track progress?
We use regular assessments, homework review, and in-session performance to track improvement. Parents receive detailed reports every month.
